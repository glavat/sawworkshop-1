7 facts about our LED-panel

Useful tool
LED-panel is a tool that helps tattoo artists to do their job better.

Designed for sketching
Workspace of the panel has size equal to A3 paper. Also, it is divided into centimeter squares. This is especially good for drawing geometric patterns.

Handmade
All panels are harvested manually at local workshop in Kyiv, combining true ukrainian love and european quality.

Frees up time
Tattoo masters draw sketches 30-60% faster through the use of panels.

Brightness control
Panel is connected to the power supply of tattoo machines via clip cord. The artist can adjust the brightness of the panel by adjusting the voltage.

Good portability
Panel is lightweight - only 1.6 kg. Therefore it’s easy and comfortable to transport.
