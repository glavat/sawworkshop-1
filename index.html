Wassup!
We are Saw Workshop's Team. We produce and sell perfect light-panels for tattooists. Artists can draw faster and more convenient with our handicraft stuff

Draw wherever you want
LED-panel is a useful tool that helps tattoo artists to draw sketches. No need to find where to draw anymore - your only place for drawing always with you.

Applicable for everyone
Each item in tattoo artist's life is really unique and non-giftable, but not a LED panel. LED panel will be the best friend for every tattooist because every tattooist draws sketches.

Handmade
All panels are handicrafted at local workshop in Kyiv, combining true Ukrainian love and European quality.

Frees up time
Tattoo masters draw sketches 30-60% faster through the use of panels.

Designed for sketching
Workspace of the panel has size equals to A3 paper. Also, it is divided into centimeter squares. This is especially good for drawing geometric patterns.

Brightness control
Panel is connected to the power supply of tattoo machines via clip cord. An artist can adjust the brightness of the panel by adjusting the voltage.
